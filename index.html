Where It All Started: Dexteria

Our flagship app Dexteria was our first step in redefining digital therapy. It is an app designed to help children strengthen fine motor skills and handwriting readiness through purposeful play. What began as a tool for therapists quickly became a trusted resource for parents and educators around the world, setting the foundation for everything we've built since.
